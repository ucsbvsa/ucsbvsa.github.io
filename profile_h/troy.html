Biography

        Hey everyone! My name is Troy Dang and I am a fourth year Communication major with a minor inPhilosophy. I’m originally from the Bay area but moved to the OC for a few years so I’ve lived all throughout most of California. Though I am a fourth year, I just transferred from Irvine Valley College (Go lasers!) this past year so this will be my second year here at UCSB. I’ve only been here a year but VSA has helped me learn and grow a lot. Because of this, I wanted to be a part of staff to help other students grow as well. As Academic Chair, I will do my best to give you as much resources as I can in order to help you progress towards the next step in life after UCSB. If you have any questions about academics or just anything you think I might know, feel free to contact me! I am always available. To share a little bit about myself, my hobbies include running around our beautiful campus, playing video games (Hearthstone, DotA2,CSGO), and watching comedies. I also listen to a lot of electronic music so if you’re into that, let’s talk! I’m super excited to be a part of staff this year and I can’t wait to have a fun-filled year with you all!
