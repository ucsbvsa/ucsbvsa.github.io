Biography

        Hey everyone! My name is Sarah Chan and I am a second year Comm major hoping to double minor in English and Professional Writing. Although I’m not Viet (Cantonese actually WHOOT WHOOT!), I decided to join VSA in the hopes of meeting amazing people and so far, being an intern has been an experience of a lifetime. Some random facts about me: I was born and raised in Arcadia, I am addicted to detective shows, and my hobbies include singing, cooking, eating, hiking, shopping, watching movies, and listening to people talk. So if you ever want to go on a food run, watch movie marathons, have jam sessions, or just talk to someone… I’m your girl! I’m a little embarrassing and a whole lot clumsy, but I promise you I’m friendly^^ So if you ever wanna chill or just need someone to lend an ear, please don’t hesitate to come talk to me/message me on FB/shoot me a text! I’ll even cook you some Chinese food if you ask nicely…
