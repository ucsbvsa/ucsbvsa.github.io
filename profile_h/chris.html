Biography

        Hey y'all I'm Chris ! I'm currently holding the seat as this year's Co-president for UCSB Vietnamese Student Association. I'm currently a 4th year Sociology major with a minor in Asian American Studies. I live in the great city of San Jose.
        I love cinematography and try to create shorts that will make others go through an array of different emotions. Besides that I enjoy my time playing my guitar, Daphne, and play tennis on the weekends. I'm excited to see the growth of the members of VSA as well as my staff. I would like to think I'm a approachable person, so don't hesitate to try and talk to me.
