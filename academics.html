Vietnamese Culture

        As Academic Chair, it is my responsibility to provide members of VSA with as much resources to allow a smooth transition into college life as well as life after college. Many times students get caught up with school and social life that they forget some of the amazing resources we have right here on campus. Members of VSA tend to be very active so while you’re at all our amazing events, you’ll have a friendly face that you can contact anytime regarding academics or work after college.

        Not only will I relay information about resources, I am also planning to provide workshops EXCLUSIVELY to VSA members only! These workshops will potentially range from resume building to sending professional emails. I will also be contacting alumni from UCSB to come give members a little information on how they are surviving life after college as well as advice on some of the things you should do while in college. VSA is an amazing club that wants to provide its members with as much resources as possible, socially and academically.

        If you have any issues or questions regarding academics or work, you will always have a friend in VSA you can come to.
