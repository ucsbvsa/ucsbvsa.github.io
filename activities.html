Activities

As your Co-Activities Chair, Kevin and Kristen organize numerous recreational events that require day trips in order to attend events such as the annual Friendship Games and three seasonal retreats. Alongside larger events, they also organize on-campus events that include creating intramural teams and hosting Fun Fitness Friday's. We are excited to take on these responsibilities and hope that VSA members will create strong bonds as their main goal is to create a family. Look forward to these VSA events this 2014-2015 year!
